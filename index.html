Mujahid Mallah web developer Practice

Mujahid Mallah ko web developer banne mein kitna time lagega, yeh unki dedication aur practice par depend karega. Agar wo consistent hain aur daily practice karte hain, to **6 se 12 mahine** mein basic skills seekh sakte hain, jaise HTML, CSS, aur JavaScript. Agar wo zyada time de sakte hain, to advanced tools aur frameworks jaise **React, Next.js** seekhne mein bhi lagbhag 1-2 saal lag sakte hain. Regular practice aur real-world projects par kaam karna unki progress ko tezi se barha sakta hai.
